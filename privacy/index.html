Privacy Policy for Social Scheduler

Last updated: November 20, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Social Scheduler ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information.

2. Information Collected

The App accesses and stores the following information solely for the purpose of providing scheduling functionality:

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens from connected social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook)
  • Basic profile information (username, account ID)
  • Video files uploaded by the user
  • Captions and metadata created by the user

3. How Information Is Used

Information is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with social media platforms via official APIs
  • Schedule and publish content on behalf of the user
  • Display scheduled posts in the App interface

4. Data Sharing

The App does not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties. Data is sent only to the official APIs of the connected platforms (TikTok, Meta) for the purpose of publishing content authorized by the user.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on infrastructure controlled by the user. Access tokens are stored securely and used only for authorized API calls.

6. Data Retention

Video files are automatically deleted 7 days after successful publishing. Access tokens are retained until the user revokes authorization.

7. User Rights

The user may revoke the App's access to their social media accounts at any time through the platform's settings, which will immediately stop all functionality.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
